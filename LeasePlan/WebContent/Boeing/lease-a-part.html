Lease a Part

Lease high-cost major components, such as flight control surfaces, engine cowlings and landing gear doors that are needed for a short duration. These high-cost major components are positioned around the world in Atlanta, Beijing, Los Angeles (Torrance), Seattle, Newark, Luxembourg, Dubai, London, and Singapore.

To Lease a Part:

Contact First Responder directly to place a lease order. Daily lease charges may be obtained by requesting a quote through the Boeing PART Page or by contacting First Responder.

Parts that can be leased are noted on the PART Page with a Lease of "Yes" and a list of lease parts can also be downloaded.

To download the list of lease parts, click on the "Download" button below.

Lease Terms

The lease term begins when the leased part leaves Boeing's dock (F.O.B. origin) and ends upon the return of the part to the store of origin at Boeing (F.O.B. Boeing) unless otherwise instructed by First Responder. F.O.B. is the same as the Incoterm D.D.P. (Delivered Duty Paid).

Lease Price

The per-day lease price is usually 1/365th of the new part price. There are no availability charges or administrative fees. There may be charges for repair and repackaging costs required to place the leased part in satisfactory condition for lease to another customer. All parts are brought up to engineering drawing specification. However, normal wear and tear is not charged.

When you are finished with the part, return the lease part to the Boeing location from which it was ordered. Please indicate "Lease Return" on the shipping/packing document(s).

If the leased part was damaged beyond economic repair (BER), over 65% of the value of the part, as determined by the Boeing Quality Assurance (QA) department, you will have to pay the price of the part. The cost of the part will be 100% if it was a new part and 95% if the leased part was previously used. If you elect to have the BER part returned to you instead of scrapped at Boeing, you are responsible to pay for freight charges.

You are responsible for lease charges until the entire leased part, including any and all of its components, are returned. That means that if a part is returned minus a component or with any component different than was originally included with the leased part at the inception of the lease term, the lease charges will continue until the component is returned or replacement is procured and installed.
