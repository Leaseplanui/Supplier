Boeing Aftermarket Solutions Offerings

  • PART Page Marketplace
  • Offers real-time inventory availability with clear pricing and part traceability online.
  • Inventory consists of new surplus, overhauled, and serviceable parts. 
  • PART Page Marketplace orders will be packed and shipped by Boeing or a Strategic Boeing Supplier with a 2 business day lead time.  To request that a part by expedited, please contact the Boeing Aftermarket Solutions Team
    aftermarketparts@boeing.com

Boeing's Aftermarket Solutions Inventory

    • Includes the Boeing warranty as specified in the Aftermarket Terms & Conditions
    • Comes with approved traceability documentation per FAA (AC) 20-62E
    • Comes with appropriate documentation and certification which includes ATA 106 Material Certification/Non-Incident Statement, Export 8130-3 (Export Certification of Airworthiness), FAA Form 8130-3 Airworthiness Approval, or JAA-1 Form (or, EASA Form), or CAAC-Tag, as required
    • Will be within the manufacturer's time, life, or usage limits
    • Was NOT procured from U.S. Government or military source
