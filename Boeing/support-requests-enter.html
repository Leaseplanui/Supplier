A Spares Material Authorization License (SMAL) can be requested when the following requirements have been met

  • At least ne aircraft operated or maintained by the customer is unable to be dispatched within 10 days, solely due to the unavailability of the part. A detailed aircraft situation, such as a "work stoppage" will also be considered.
  • The part is a Boeing or Douglas model proprietary detail/component part built per engineering drawing only and is not an assembly, installation, kit, supplier proprietary part, undrilled part, noted condition part, repair, wire bundle, raw material, or a part made using alternate materials and or processes not already called out in the engineering drawing.
  • A current insurance cerificate is on file with Boeing
  • Customer has perviously executed a Boeing CSGTA, HMSGTA, or MRO-A agreement.
  • Customer agrees to not use any Boeing proprietar materials and information provided or licensedin connection with granting the SMAL request for any purpose other than the manufacture of a single ship set needed to return the identified AOG aircraft back to service.
  • Customer has part fabrication services available to manufacture a new part by the specified return to service data

To Begin a SMAL Request

Place an AOG priority purchase order for at least one ship set of a part to Boeing and then click the "I accept" button below

SMAL Terms and Conditions

Accepting the Terms and Conditions, you (the Customer) are authorized to use BPI and BPM as described in the Terms and COnditions, and Boeing will provide to Customer BPI and BPM through normal channels in support of this autorization. By clicking on the "I Accept" button, you agree to these Terms and Conditions.
