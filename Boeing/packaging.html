Packaging

Spare parts are packaged in conformance to ATA Specification No. 300 Specification for Packaging of Airline Supplies.

Major repairable parts are packaged one each in reusable Category II containers. Expendable parts are packaged for compatibility with item characteristics and anticipated use.

Boeing packaging protects parts for all modes of transportation.

AOG and Critical shipping containers will be identified by markings on the container exteriors to make them easier to locate in your receiving area
